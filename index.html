Simple, honest policies for a simple, honest app. Your files stay private, while we use standard industry tools to keep the app running.

01
Privacy Principle

Flow is built on the principle that your files are yours. We do not upload or transmit any of the documents or files you import into the app. Everything you open, scan, or save is processed entirely on your device.

02
Data Collection

To provide our services, Flow utilizes third-party SDKs that collect certain information. This includes Device Identifiers (such as Advertising IDs), App Interactions, and Diagnostics/Crash Logs. This data is used for analytics, advertising performance, and ensuring the technical stability of the app.

03
Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

• Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements. It collects device IDs to personalize ads and measure performance.
• Google ML Kit: Used for on-device QR and barcode scanning. It may collect diagnostic data to improve scanning accuracy.

04
Data Security

All data transmitted by our third-party SDKs is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS). We do not provide a custom account system, so no personal login credentials are ever stored by us.

05
Local Storage

Files you import are stored in Flow's local app directory on your device. This data is removed if you uninstall the app. You are responsible for maintaining backups of your important files.

07

Changes to This Policy
