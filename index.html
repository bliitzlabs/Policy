Simple, honest policies for a simple, honest app. Everything stays on your device.

01
Privacy Policy

Flow is built on the principle that your files are yours. We do not collect, upload, or transmit any of the files you import into the app. Everything you open or save stays entirely on your device.

02
Data We Do Not Collect

Flow does not collect personal information, usage analytics, crash reports sent to third parties, or any identifiable data. There are no accounts, no logins, and no cloud sync. Your files never leave your device unless you explicitly share them yourself.

03
Local Storage

Files you import are copied into Flow's local app storage directory. This is stored only on your device and is removed if you uninstall the app. Scan history and pinned files are stored locally using your device's standard storage mechanisms.

04
Third-Party Services

Flow does not integrate with any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking services. Any ads displayed within the app are served locally and do not profile or track your behaviour.

05
QR Code Scanner

QR codes scanned through Flow are processed entirely on-device. Scan results are saved locally in your scan history and are never sent to any external server. You can delete your scan history at any time from within the app.

06
Terms of Use

Flow is provided as-is for personal use. You agree not to use the app for any unlawful purpose or in any way that could harm others. We reserve the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

07
Disclaimer

While we take care to ensure Flow works reliably, we cannot guarantee it is error-free at all times. We are not liable for any loss of data or damages arising from use of the app. Always keep backups of files that are important to you.

08
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on our website and, where significant, communicated through the app. The date of the latest revision will always be visible at the bottom of this page.
